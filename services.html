HOW WE WORK

Our proposition is simple: we deliver meaningful social impact whilst helping students gain invaluable consulting experience. Financial barriers often prevent social organisations from receiving advisory services regarding critical areas like business strategy, market competitiveness, and technology implementation.

180 Degrees Consulting at the University of Toronto is dedicated to filling this gap in a mutually-beneficial effort with students. We help Toronto's social organisations deliver more impact to those who need it most, all while giving students the opportunity to further their own skills in support of genuine causes. Crucially, our services are priced accessibly.

Our typical workflow is as follows:

  • Scope out engagements
  • Consultants carry out engagements, receiving professional mentorship and producing work-in-progress documents
  • Final deliverables are presented and transferred
